What is DriveSafe AI?

DriveSafe AI is an intelligent platform for predictive vehicle safety, designed to anticipate mechanical failures, detect risky driver behavior, and ensure optimal fleet performance—without relying on any extra hardware.

Key Features

  • Predictive Maintenance & Failure Forecasting
  • Driver Behavior Monitoring & Risk Scoring
  • Conversational AI for Service Assistance
  • Fleet Health and Uptime Intelligence
  • EV Battery Diagnostics and Charging Insights

Who is it for?

DriveSafe AI is designed for OEMs like Maruti Suzuki, shared mobility platforms, electric vehicle fleets, and servicing networks aiming to boost reliability, efficiency, and customer trust.
